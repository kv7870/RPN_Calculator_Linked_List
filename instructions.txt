Welcome to Kevin's RPN calculator

To perform an operation, enter the operand after the operators.

For example, to perform 10/2: 
	1) type "10" and press enter
	2) type "2/" and press enter, OR enter "2" and "/" separately.

Supported operators: + - * / ^
To delete the top element, type 'd' and press enter.
To clear the entire stack, type 'c' and press enter.
